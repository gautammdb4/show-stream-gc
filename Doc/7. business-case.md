## üöÄ Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### Technical Risks

#### High-Priority Risks
1. **Scalability Challenges**
   - **Risk:** System performance degradation under high load
   - **Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** High
   - **Mitigation:** Comprehensive load testing, auto-scaling, performance monitoring

2. **Integration Complexity**
   - **Risk:** Microservices communication failures
   - **Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** Medium
   - **Mitigation:** Circuit breakers, retry logic, event sourcing patterns

3. **Data Consistency Issues**
   - **Risk:** Eventual consistency challenges in distributed system
   - **Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** High
   - **Mitigation:** Saga patterns, compensating transactions, monitoring

#### Medium-Priority Risks
1. **Third-party Dependencies**
   - **Risk:** Payment gateway or cloud service outages
   - **Mitigation:** Multiple provider integration, fallback mechanisms

2. **Security Vulnerabilities**
   - **Risk:** Data breaches or payment security issues
   - **Mitigation:** Security audits, PCI compliance, encryption

### Market Risks

#### Competitive Response

- **Probability:** High | **Impact:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Continuous innovation, patent applications, first-mover advantage

#### Market Adoption
